• Getting Started
    • What do I do if I forgot my password?

      The main screen has 3 sections:

      • Statement (large box on left side of screen) **remember, this is a snapshot of your account as of the statement date so any payments received after statement date may not appear on the statement.

        • Your total balance duea ppears in bold print at the top of the box (as of statement date)

        • Your current statement appears in this box

        • You can view your statement by clicking on the “Print” link at the top of the box

        • You can view your invoice by clicking on the invoice number

        • You can view both your invoice and your billing detail by clicking on the PDF icon next to the invoice number

      • Invoices (top right box)

        • This section lists all of your invoices, the original amount, and amount due.
          **this is where you can see if/what invoices have outstanding amounts

        • Click on any invoice number to view, print or save

      • Account Activity (bottom right box)

        • This section allows you to view all transactions on your account.
